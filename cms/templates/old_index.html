Resourcing Your Success

Biotech Start-Up Management (BSUM) is a one-stop service provider for life science ventures. BSUM expert services help you with all the steps from innovation management to goal oriented international business development. BSUM expert management, network of trusted partners and pool of life science professionals guarantee the high quality of our services fitting best to your current needs.

Our customers are mainly pharmaceutical and biotech companies, research institutes and business acceleration units.
